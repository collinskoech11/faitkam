About Us

We Love Fresh Organic Food

Buy From Across The World

Welcome to FaitKam Foods, your number one source for all things fresh farm produce. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of Kenyan farm produce, with a focus on grains and Pulses, Fruits and Vegetables, Herbs and spices,and Kenyan grown flowers.

Founded in 2015 by our founder; Mr.Julius Ithai, FaitKam Foods has come a long way from its beginnings in Meru, Kenya. When our founder first started out, his passion for producing and distributing farm produce drove him to do tons of research on the production of quality farm produce and the local and international distribution of farm produce across the world so that FaitKam Foods can offer you easy access to quality farm produce besides your location around the world.

We now serve customers all over the world , and are thrilled that we're able to turn our passion into our own website. We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
